Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I get started with the platform?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, choose your plan, and you'll be guided through the setup process. We also offer onboarding sessions to help you get the most out of the platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features of the platform. No credit card is required to start your trial.

How secure is my data?

Data security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular backups, and secure data centers to protect your information. Our platform is fully compliant with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

What integrations do you support?

Our platform integrates with popular services like Google Analytics, Mailchimp, Shopify, WordPress, Zapier, and many more. We also offer an API for custom integrations.
